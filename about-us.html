About Us

Initially conceptualized at Startup Weekend Pittsburgh in the fall of 2012 as "Academica," Scholar Hero began as a conversation between several individuals interested in making a difference in education by helping scholars professionalize themselves online. Several months later, Scholar Hero, Inc. officially filed for S-corporation status.

Incubation

Starting in 2013, Scholar Hero was accepted as one of the top applications for incubation at The Hustle Den, Pittsburgh’s newest incubation space, which is organized and managed by the Thrill Mill, Inc. – a Pennsylvania non-profit organization currently preparing its federal 501(c)(3) filing status.

Currently headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Scholar Hero benefits from the modest cost of living as well as being surrounded by several of the top technical and research universities in the nation (e.g. Carnegie Mellon University and the University of Pittsburgh).

Our goals, of course, are ACADEMIC:

  • Advocate the concerns of all scholars
  • Constructive feedback when one is brave to expose their work
  • Appreciate the works of those who produce it
  • Distinguish scholars who have earned their reputation as expect
  • Explore new ways to better serve the scholarly community
  • Mentorship should be a privilege, not a burden
  • Inspired scholars should never doubt themselves
  • Create spaces for scholars to meet, share, and collaborate safely and productively

Now it is a company whose ambition is certainly great, yet we are vigilant in our mission to make a real difference in academic culture. The issues scholars face every day require serious thought, thus we must match the level of commitment that scholars have in their own work in order to succeed.
